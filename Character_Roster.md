# Character Roster

## Season 1: The Digital Squeeze (Maya Brooks)

### Protagonist
*   **Maya Brooks:** 24, Logistics Coordinator turned Entrepreneur. "The Elite Asset."
    *   **Traits:** Sharp, aesthetic-focused (beige cloud couch), in debt ($42k student loans), transitioning from passive consumer to active owner.
    *   **Arc:** Escaping the corporate "Panopticon" to start her own business ("The Curated Closet").

### Allies
*   **Imani:** Maya's best friend and former coworker.
    *   **Role:** The Business Brain. Introduced the FIRE movement and AI tools.
    *   **Status:** Fired, currently living with Dante, working retail/Uber while building the startup.
*   **Jaylen:** Maya's brother.
    *   **Role:** The Tech Wizard. Provides the "Red Pill" on AI risks vs. rewards.
    *   **Traits:** Gamer, cynical but pragmatic.
*   **Dante:** Imani's boyfriend.
    *   **Role:** Supportive partner. (Renamed from David to avoid conflict).

### Love Interests
*   **Marcus Turner:** 42, Truck Owner-Operator.
    *   **Role:** The Physical Connection / The Complication.
    *   **Traits:** "Zane-style" intensity, secretive about family, helps with logistics but brings chaos.
*   **David:** The Thrift Store Guy (Chapter 2).
    *   **Role:** The "One That Got Away." Represents authentic connection Maya wasn't ready for.
*   **Caleb:** The "Noir Detective" (Chapter 2).
    *   **Role:** The Simulation. Used AI to date; unmatched by Maya.

### The Antagonists (The System)
*   **Aria:** The AI Assistant / Surveillance Tool.
    *   **Role:** The Voice of the Panopticon. Friendly but oppressive.
*   **Henderson:** Maya's Manager.
    *   **Role:** The Middleman. Enforces the algorithm's will while being threatened by it himself.
*   **Katherine:** CEO.
    *   **Role:** The Face of Capital. Delivers the "Efficiency" speeches.

### Supporting / Background
*   **Kevin:** Coworker (formerly Jayden). "Quiet Quitter" working two jobs.
*   **Sarah:** Fired coworker. Represents the human cost of automation (single mom context).

## Season 2: The Physical Extraction (Marcus Turner) - Prequel

### Protagonist
*   **Marcus Turner:** 42, Truck Owner-Operator. "The King of the Road."
    *   **Traits:** Old school, paid in cash, physically fit, skeptical of tech but reliant on it.
    *   **Arc:** Realizing his "ownership" is an illusion in the face of autonomous fleets.

### Family ( The "Secret" Drama)
*   **Trina:** Baby Mama #1. "The Fit One."
    *   **Role:** The pressure. Demands child support for their son (Junior).
*   **Junior:** Marcus's Son.
    *   **Role:** The innocent. Wants tech/toys Marcus can barely afford.
*   **Keisha:** Baby Mama #2. (To be developed).

### The Antagonists
*   **The Ghost Fleet:** Autonomous trucks (Tesla/Waymo/Aurora).
*   **The Dispatchers/Brokers:** The humans implementing the replacement.
